Return & Refund Policy

At AntsTech, we prioritize your satisfaction. If you are not happy with your purchase, you can return the products within 7 days from the date of delivery. Please note that returns and exchanges are subject to the following terms and conditions:

General Returns & Exchanges

Products must be returned in their original, unopened packaging, including all accessories, manuals, warranty cards, and brand seals.

Activated IT and Telecom products are not eligible for return.

Online purchases can only be returned or refunded if the product remains unopened.

Opened items can be returned but will incur a restocking fee.

Customers requesting unopened units must sign an acknowledgment waiving further claims against AntsTech.

A valid original sales invoice is required for all returns or exchanges.

Products must be unused, unopened, and include all original packaging materials, accessories, manuals, and promotional vouchers.

Sending an email to support@antstech.com

Reaching out via WhatsApp at +961 70 88 78 05

Non-Returnable & Non-Exchangeable Items
  • Incomplete packaging or damaged/scratched products.
  • Customized or special-order products, cut cables/wires, apparel, and personal care/hygiene products.
  • Scratched or redeemed online activation codes.
  • Devices with activation/password locks, such as Find My iPhone (FMIP), Find My Mobile (FMM), or Find My Device (FMD).
  • If VAT has been refunded against the invoice.
Product Inspection & Proof of Delivery (POD)

Customers must inspect products before signing the POD.

Products reported damaged after signing the POD will not be eligible for return or warranty claims.

Abuse of Return Policy

AntsTech reserves the right to issue warnings, impose restrictions, or terminate customer accounts if:

  • Excessive returns are made
  • Products are frequently returned in non-original condition.
  • Orders are frequently canceled
